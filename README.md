## 👋 About Me

Hi! I am **Huiyao Zhang (张晖耀)**, currently a **Master's student in Computer Technology at the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS)**, supervised by **Prof. Ye Li**. I am also a **research intern at TeleAI**, working with **Chuyi Wang**. My research focuses on **Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs)**, **Visual Agents**, and **efficient foundation architectures** such as **Mamba / SSM**.

More recently, I have been particularly interested in **multimodal reasoning**, **MLLM post-training**, and how **visual evidence can be more reliably consumed by the final decision layer**. Before joining UCAS, I received my bachelor's degree in **Mechatronic Engineering from Guangxi University**.

My previous work spans multimodal understanding, promptable foundation model adaptation, cross-modal reasoning, topology-aware sequence modeling, and intelligent visual systems. I have submitted multiple papers to venues such as **AAAI, CVPR, ECCV, and IJCAI**, and published research in journal and conference venues related to intelligent vision and multimodal AI.

## 📝 Selected Publications and Projects

### Ongoing / Recent Work

- **IP-SAM: Prompt-Space Conditioning for Prompt-Absent Camouflaged Object Detection**  
  First author. Focused on adapting promptable foundation models to prompt-absent automatic segmentation settings, including method design, implementation, and paper writing.  
  Links: [Homepage Publications](https://zhang1huiyao.github.io/zhanghuiyao/#publications) · [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.27250)

- **MG-COUB: Unified Multi-Granularity Reasoning for Multi-Task Camouflaged Object Understanding**  
  Co-first author. Worked on model architecture, core implementation, and paper writing, with emphasis on fine-grained reasoning under camouflage.

- **ArgusCogito: Chain-of-Thought for Cross-Modal Synergy and Omnidirectional Reasoning in Camouflaged Object Segmentation**  
  Co-first / major contributor. Focused on multimodal agent-style reasoning, prompt design, and end-to-end experimental validation.  
  Links: [Project Page](https://zhang1huiyao.github.io/ArgusCogito/) · [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2508.18050)

- **Fluxamba: Topology-Aware Anisotropic State Space Models for Geological Lineament Segmentation in Multi-Source Remote Sensing**  
  Major contributor. Contributed to Mamba-style architecture design, data engine construction, and topology-aware modeling.  
  Link: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.17288)

- **Bridging the Geometry Mismatch: Frequency-Aware Anisotropic Serialization for Thin-Structure SSMs**  
  Major contributor. Worked on structured visual modeling for thin-structure understanding with efficient sequence architectures.  
  Link: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.28503)
